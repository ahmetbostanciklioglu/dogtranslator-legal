Privacy Policy

DogTranslator AI

Privacy designed in — we collect as little as possible.

Effective date: June 4, 2026 · Applies to the DogTranslator AI iOS app.

Overview

We respect your privacy. This policy explains what DogTranslator AI (the “App”) collects, why, and the choices you have. The App is designed to collect as little as possible, and most information stays on your device.

Microphone & audio

When you tap “Listen,” the App accesses your microphone to analyze sound in real time and estimate barks and mood. This analysis happens on your device. We do not record, store, upload, or transmit your audio. Microphone access is used only while you are actively using the listening feature.

Photos

If you choose a photo (e.g. for breed, age, or mood scanning, or your dog’s profile), it is processed to produce a result. Photos are used on-device for the feature you requested and are not sold or used for advertising. We request photo access only when you use a feature that needs it.

Dog profile

Information you enter — your dog’s name, breed, age, size, traits, and photo — is stored on your device to personalize translations. It is optional, and you can edit or remove it at any time in Settings.

Purchases

Subscriptions are processed by Apple through the App Store. We receive transaction status (for example, active or expired) so we can unlock premium features. We never receive your full payment card details.

Analytics & diagnostics

If enabled, we may use privacy-respecting, aggregated diagnostics to fix crashes and improve the App. This data does not identify you personally. We do not sell your personal data, and we do not use it for cross-app tracking.

Third-party services

  • Apple / App Store & StoreKit — handles purchases and subscription status.
  • Dog CEO API — used to fetch sample breed reference images for the scan features. Requests do not include your personal data.

These services have their own privacy practices, which we encourage you to review.

Data retention & your control

Most data lives only on your device and is removed when you delete the App. You can reset your dog profile in Settings at any time. To request information about data associated with you, contact us at the address below.

Children

DogTranslator AI is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect their personal data.

Changes & contact

We may update this policy and will revise the effective date above. Questions or requests? Contact privacy@dogtranslator.app (support: support@dogtranslator.app).
